Our Story

The idea behind Maison de Glace was to make sophisticated, handcrafted French-style ice cream accessible to all dessert enthusiasts. We combine tradition and contemporary flavours to create scoops that tell a story, drawing inspiration from the classic charm of Parisian patisseries and the cosiness of summertime memories.

Our Mission

We are ready to improve any dessert occasion, whether it is a noon treat or a celebratory scoop. Each flavour is created with care, affection, and high-quality ingredients. Maison de Glace is more than just ice cream; it is a taste of luxury in every tub.

What Makes Us Different?

  • Handmade in small batches
  • Natural ingredients only
  • Unique French-inspired flavours
  • Premium tubs with beautiful presentation
